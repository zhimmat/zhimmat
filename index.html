Billiards?

Billiards is a josh agle inspired piece that was designed in his style of work. Josh agle is a very inspiring artist that has one of the most eye catching styles of drawing people and their environment.

Track em

Have you ever asked yourself "Where is my bus?" Living in Ottawa I've always asked myself that question so I decided to come up with an app that allows the users to track their bus and to be able to see it in real time. With that being said, users are now able to open their phone, click a certain bus number, and the app will open a GPS showing you that bus.

SmartPint

SmartPint is an app that allows bartenders to regulate consumers alcohol consumption and not only stop them from over drinking but also stop themselves from losing their beloved jobs. It was designed to help the user keep tabs on everyone in the bar in an efficient manner, taking into consideration how busy some bars get nowadays.

Stamena

Stamena is an app and a wearable that allows athletes to track their stamina at the end of their game to analyze how well their body is doing physically. Stamena also helps the users improve during their games by notifying them of when they're burning too much stamina.
